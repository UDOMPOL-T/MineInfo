They add new content Mods can add new mobs, items, dimensions, structures, recipes, and more. This gives players new things to discover, challenges to overcome, and ways to have fun with the game.

Mods allow players to customize the game to fit their playstyle. They can adjust the difficulty, add new mechanics, change the graphics, and more.
